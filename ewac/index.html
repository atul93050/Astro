EWAC was started as a joint venture between Larsen & Toubro Limited, India & Eutectic Castolin Group, Switzerland, in 1962. In the year 2011, EWAC Alloys Limited became a wholly owned subsidiary of Larsen & Toubro Limited (L&T). Subsequently, in 2018, EWAC was acquired by Colfax, USA. EWAC Alloys Limited has been a leader in Maintenance and Repair in the Indian Welding Industry. The company manufactures and sells cutting-edge welding products, kits, and accessories. EWAC exports products to over 50 countries across South America, Europe, Africa, ME, SEA, China, Japan, South Korea, Sri Lanka, and Bangladesh.

Why Choose

For over 64+ years, EWAC has been a trusted partner to industries operating in the wear-intensive and heavy-duty sectors. Customers choose EWAC for our innovative welding products and solutions that improve productivity, reduce maintenance costs, and extend component life. Backed by strong technical expertise and a customer-focused approach, EWAC continues to deliver reliable, high-performance solutions built on Technology, Track record, and Trust.

EWAC's dedicated Research & Development team continuously develops innovative welding technologies and customized solutions to meet evolving industrial challenges.

EWAC offers specialized training programs and technical workshops to enhance welding skills, application knowledge, and industry best practices for the engineers, customers and professionals.

EWAC provides end-to-end turnkey maintenance and repair solutions on-site or through our reclamation facility, offer technical support to enhance equipment life and operational efficiency.

For over 64 years, EWAC has built a strong legacy of trust, innovation, and technical excellence in maintenance and repair welding products and solutions across diverse industrial sectors.

With a strong nationwide presence, EWAC provides prompt technical support, product availability, and customer service through its extensive application support and distribution network.
